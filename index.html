Who I Am

Bridging Engineering Innovation with Data Intelligence

A mechanical engineer and data analyst who builds renewable energy systems for the future. My work is where physical hardware meets digital intelligence. I design, model, and optimize integrated systems, ensuring they operate with maximum efficiency and reliability.

My Expertise

I bring a holistic approach to renewable energy, using modern tools to design and manage systems from the ground up.


Renewable System Design & Integration

I specialize in designing cohesive energy ecosystems that combine different sources—like solar and micro-hydro—with energy storage to create a stable and resilient power supply.


3D Modeling and Simulation

Before any physical construction begins, I build and test entire systems as detailed "digital twins." This allows me to simulate real-world conditions, validate designs, and optimize performance virtually.


Machine Learning for Optimization

I apply machine learning models for predictive analytics. This means forecasting energy generation based on weather patterns, predicting demand, and automating energy flow to ensure power is distributed where it's needed most, right when it's needed.


Data-Driven Performance Analysis

I use data to understand not just how a system is performing, but why. This allows for predictive maintenance, preventing failures before they happen and extending the life of critical components.

What Drives Me

I'm passionate about building resilient, eco-friendly mobility solutions for emerging markets. From converting conventional vehicles to electric powertrains to designing modular platforms for rural areas, I tackle real-world challenges with innovative engineering approaches.
